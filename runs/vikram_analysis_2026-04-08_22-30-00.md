### Issue 8: "Hey, are you still there" — English Silence Handling
- **Frequency:** 5/202 total; **3/10 Google Sheet (v2) transcripts**

- **Still present in v2?** YES -- this is a high-priority unresolved issue

  > [8/4/2026, 3:03:37 pm] "assistant: Hey, are you still there"
- **Proposed fix:** The current prompt has `"Are you there?" | Say "हाँ sir"` but the Bolna platform's built-in silence handler sends "Hey, are you still there" in English. This needs to be overridden in the prompt AND potentially in Bolna's agent config.
  - **Before:** `| "Are you there?" | Say "हाँ sir" and **rephrase** (never repeat verbatim) whatever you were asking. |`
  - **After:** `| "Are you there?" / Silence detected | Say "हाँ sir, मैं यहाँ हूँ" and **rephrase** (never repeat verbatim) whatever you were asking. **NEVER say "Hey, are you still there" or any English silence prompt.** Always use Hinglish: "Sir, सुन रहे हैं?" or "Sir, आप वहाँ हैं?" This step is important. |`

  **Note:** This may also require configuring Bolna's silence detection message in the agent settings (not just the prompt). Check the Bolna dashboard for "silence message" or "idle prompt" settings.

## Best Practice Gaps

### From bolna-prompting-guide.md:
1. **Intelligent Hangup**: The prompt does not mention configuring Bolna's "Hangup using a prompt" feature. Consider adding hangup conditions (e.g., after Branch C close, after two failed silence checks) to the Bolna agent configuration.
2. **Post-Call Extraction**: No extraction classifiers are defined in the prompt for downstream CRM automation. Consider documenting what fields should be extracted (interest level, required voltage, capacity, next steps).

### From elevenlabs-conversational-ai.md:
1. **Error Handling Section**: While the prompt has "Tool Failure Recovery", it could benefit from a dedicated `# Error Handling` section header (models pay extra attention to specific headings per ElevenLabs docs).
2. **Targeted Refinement**: The best practice of "modify one element at a time" should guide future prompt iterations.

### From elevenlabs-tts-optimization.md:
1. **Pronunciation Dictionary**: For consistently mispronounced terms (iTarang, Trontek), consider uploading a .PLS pronunciation dictionary to ElevenLabs rather than relying solely on prompt instructions.
2. **Phone Number Formatting**: The prompt says "digit by digit" but the TTS guide recommends commas between groups for pausing: "nine nine seven, zero zero zero, one two three" vs "nine nine seven zero zero zero one two three".
  - **Before:** `- Phone numbers digit by digit: 997 → "nine nine seven"`
  - **After:** `- Phone numbers digit by digit with commas for pausing: 997-000 → "nine nine seven, zero zero zero"`
